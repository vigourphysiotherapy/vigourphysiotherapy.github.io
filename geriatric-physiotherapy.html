Geriatric Physiotherapy


As we get older, our metabolism slows down, and several underlying problems begin to become prominent. To prevent this or to manage pre-existing symptoms, Geriatric Physiotherapy can be essential in maintaining health in our later years. Geriatric Physiotherapy helps us use our muscles in such a way that helps us work efficiently and decrease the likelihood of injuries in old age.

Advantages of Geriatric Physiotherapy;

  • Restore mobility, strength, and endurance
  • Reduce chronic pain
  • Improve health and fitness
  • Improve bad posture
  • Work with physical limitations.
